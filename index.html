Autonomous Pesticide Robot

It helps in overcoming the risks and efficiencies in pesticide sprayingallied with labor shortages. It utilizes an RFID sensor, cameras, andweather monitoring tools while navigating the fields on its own; suchmeasures are efficient for precise chemical application. Gains fromthe use of the Autonomous Pesticide Spraying Robot include minimalwastage of chemicals, healthier crops, and fewer dangers faced byfarmers from pesticides. There will be accuracy regarding weatherconditions that favor diseases in crops; thus, there will be timelyinterventions with the improvement of yields. It is a scalabilitycosteffective solution for sustainable modernization in agriculture.

STEM Borer Detection

STEM BORER explores critical challenges in modern agriculture, including pest& disease attacks, climate change, and resource scarcity. It highlights theimpact of stem borers on crop yields and evaluates existing solutions likepesticides and light traps, emphasizing their limitations. The proposed StemBorer Detector offers a real-time, low-cost, and automated approach to pestdetection using wingbeat frequency analysis, environmental monitoring, andinstant alerts. The presentation underscores the need for innovative, techdriven solutions to enhance agricultural sustainability.
